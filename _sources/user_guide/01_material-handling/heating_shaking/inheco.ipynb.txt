    "    - PLR name: `inheco_thermoshake_rm`\n",
    "    - Cat. no.: 7100144\n",
    "    - status: PLR-tested\n",
    "    - shaking orbit = 2.0 mm  \n",
    "    - shaking speed = 100 - 2000 rpm\n",
